# One Minute Pitch

Miro Supply is a decision-support demo for factory operators.

We load a synthetic battery factory, inject a supply-chain shock, build the supply graph, simulate the cascade, and generate an operational risk brief.

Instead of showing a generic dashboard, the system answers the practical question:

**What breaks first, how urgent is it, and what should I do now?**

The brief shows:

- the primary failure chain
- the most constrained node
- the estimated disruption window
- recommended mitigation actions

Then we interrogate the system to explain why the risk escalated, so the output feels explainable instead of black-box.

We built this on top of an existing simulation engine, but our contribution is the factory-risk framing, the battery supply-chain scenario system, the graph grounding pack, the operational brief workflow, and the demo-safe execution bundle.

So instead of reacting to supply shocks with spreadsheets and panic, a factory operator gets a fast, explainable operational risk brief.
