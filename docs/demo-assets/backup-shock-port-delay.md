Simulate an operational cascade for the factory scenario in this document.

Shock event:
A port disruption delays inbound battery materials by 14 days. Ocean arrivals are unreliable, rerouting adds cost, and expedited freight capacity is limited.

Model the reactions of procurement, plant operations, logistics, suppliers, customers, and regulators as a causal operational system, not a public-opinion simulation.

Track and reason about:

- inventory burn
- transport delay propagation
- production throughput risk
- downtime / line-down risk
- cost pressure from rerouting and expedite freight
- customer delivery risk

The output should emphasize:

1. the primary failure chain
2. the estimated disruption window
3. the most constrained node in the system
4. the best mitigation actions to buy time
5. concise, grounded reasoning from the simulated stakeholders
